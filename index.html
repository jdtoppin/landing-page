About Me

Hey, I'm Josiah and this is my corner of the internet. I live in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. During the winter, I often wonder why I live here given the air hurts. Anyways, my wife, our three cats, and a dog all live in central Edmonton. We enjoy checking out the festivals and new food options that the city has to offer. I am a type 1 Diabetic and have been on a CGM/pump setup for over a year now. I quickly transitioned to what is called "Looping" using an iOS application called Trio. Read more about that over here.
