About Me

I live in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. My wife, our three cats, and a dog all live in central Edmonton. We enjoy checking out the festivals and new food options that the city has to offer. We also love heading to the mountains in the warmer months of the year.

As you can tell, I dabble in photography. I shoot with a Leica Q typ 116.

I am a type 1 Diabetic and have been on a CGM/pump setup for over a year now. I quickly transitioned to what is called "Looping" using an iOS application called Trio. Read more about that over here.

The blog is about our travels, projects I'm working on, politics, diabetes, photography, gaming, food and other musings about life.
